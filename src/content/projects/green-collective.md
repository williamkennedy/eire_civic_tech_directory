Green Collective supplies data and insights to understand the power sector in Ireland. It collates multiple public sources of renewable, storage, and fossil fuel generation data from 2019 onwards, offers real-time estimates of Irish utility-scale solar generation, and promotes transparency around the Irish grid's decarbonisation efforts.
