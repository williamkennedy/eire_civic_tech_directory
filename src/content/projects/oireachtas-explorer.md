Oireachtas Explorer turns the public record of the Houses of the Oireachtas into something you can actually explore. Browse the Dáil and Seanad across every historical session, dig into member profiles and voting histories, follow bills through the legislative process, and read full debate transcripts and save citations — all from one fast, searchable interface.
