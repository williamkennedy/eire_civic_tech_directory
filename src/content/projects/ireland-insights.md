Most civic maps stop at the county line. IrelandInsights doesn't — every layer, from Census 2022 demographics and historical 1926 records to rental prices, BER ratings, and General and Local Election results, is explorable right down to your own Electoral Division. For campaign teams, the Analyst-tier Canvass Planner goes further still, ranking areas by modelled support and transfer opportunity using real GE 2024 and verified LE 2024 data. It's built on genuine election results and census figures, not live vote counts — Ireland doesn't publish results at Electoral Division level, and IrelandInsights won't pretend otherwise.
