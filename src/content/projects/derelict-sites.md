A data-led project focused on vacancy and dereliction in Ireland as part of the wider housing and town-centre crisis. The platform examines the scale and impact of derelict and long-term vacant properties by converting fragmented statutory registers into a clear, map-based resource. It tracks locations, enforcement steps, and estimated values across Cork County Council, Cork City Council, Dublin City Council, Limerick City & County Council, and Mayo County Council. The register includes photos and Google Street View links to make records easier to understand, along with useful links and available grants explaining the Derelict Sites Process Workflow.
