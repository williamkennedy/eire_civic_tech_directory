A civic-data pipeline for Irish parliamentary transparency. Dáil Tracker ingests, joins, and analyses public records about Irish elected representatives and the state around them — attendance, legislation, questions, votes, payments, declared interests, lobbying contacts, procurement awards, political finance, and judicial appointments — and presents them in one searchable dashboard and a read-only JSON API.
