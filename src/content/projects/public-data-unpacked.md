Public Data Unpacked turns Irish public-sector datasets into clear, interactive dashboards. Each page brings a single dataset into focus — filtering, summarising, and visualising the figures behind the headlines. Datasets include IPAS arrivals and deportations, emergency accommodation, PPS allocations, Live Register by nationality, welfare recipients, and more.
